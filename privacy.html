Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 3, 2026

LenzIQ is developed by Halto Apps. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

LenzIQ is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide the service.

  • Images: When you use the scan feature, the image you capture is sent to our server for AI analysis. Images are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Scan history: Your scan results are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your scan history to any server.
  • Device language & settings: We read your device's language setting to provide localized responses. This data does not leave your device.
  • Purchase information: If you subscribe to LenzIQ Pro, purchases are handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze the image you capture and return an AI-generated result (weight, measurement, count, color or plant identification).
  • To deliver the app in your preferred language.
  • To verify your subscription status through the App Store or Google Play.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the core functionality of LenzIQ.

3. Third-Party Services

LenzIQ uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Gemini API: Images you capture are sent to Google's Gemini API for AI-powered analysis. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway: Our backend server is hosted on Railway. No personal data is stored on Railway infrastructure.
  • RevenueCat (if applicable): Used to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase data per their Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

We do not retain images or scan results on our servers. All processing is ephemeral — images are analyzed and immediately discarded. Scan history exists solely on your device and can be deleted at any time from within the app.

5. Children's Privacy

LenzIQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we do not store personal data on our servers, there is effectively no data to access or delete. For any concerns, please contact us directly.

7. Security

All communication between the LenzIQ app and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS. We implement industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
